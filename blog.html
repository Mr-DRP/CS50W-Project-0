How Businesses are Leveraging Digital Marketing

Digital marketing helps your business to grow, either it is small or big business can leverage digital marketing. It provides endless opportunities for businesses and customers to engage with each other.

Digital marketing is the module of marketing that uses the internet and online-based digital technologies such as desktop computers, mobile phones, and other digital media and platforms to promote products, websites, and services. Digital marketing and Inbound marketing are words that get thrown around a lot. Digital marketing is a part of the scope which is Inbound marketing.
Digital marketing strategy includes conducting market research using digital marketing research tools such as Google Keyword Planner, analyzing marketing reports using digital analytics software such as Adobe Analytics, and launching digital display advertisements using digital advertising platforms such as Google Marketing Platform.

Some digital marketing Channels

  • Email marketing
  • Organic Search
  • Paid Search
  • Social Media Channels
  • Affiliate Channels
An example of online marketing is launching a Facebook advertisement campaign to promote products, and during this campaign, collecting personal details of leads such as emails to follow-up through email.
